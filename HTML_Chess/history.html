Chess has a long history and is said to date back as much as 1000 years. The earliest form of Chess originated in India during the 6th century. Chess is a very unique game in that it does not take a lot of knowledge to play but could take a lifetime of knowledge to master. Chess is all about planning your attacks and defenses and always knowing what you're gonna do five moves ahead of your opponent.
